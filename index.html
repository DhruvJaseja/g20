G20 Bharat 2023

Theme: One Earth, One Family, One Future

The G20 summit's presidency was held by India in 2023, and it hosted various meetings and forums among the world's top 20 economies.

The main summit took place in New Delhi's Pragati Maidan on September 9th and 10th, with heads of state from around the world in attendance.

The theme of the G20 Bharat 2023 summit is "One Earth, One Family, One Future."

This theme reflects a vision of unity and collaboration among the world's top 20 economies to address global challenges and build a better future for all.

It emphasizes the importance of:

  • One Earth: Focusing on sustainable development and environmental conservation to protect our planet for future generations.
  • One Family: Promoting inclusivity, equality, and a sense of global community where all nations work together as one family.
  • One Future: Working towards a shared future that is prosperous, peaceful, and built on mutual cooperation.

The G20 summit provides a platform for leaders to discuss and implement policies in alignment with this theme, addressing critical global issues and striving for a better future for humanity.

Information on key sessions and discussions

The G20 Summit 2023 in New Delhi featured a diverse agenda with discussions on various global issues. Some of the key topics and agenda items included:

  1. Global Economy: Strategies for fostering global economic growth, stability, and recovery.
  2. Cryptocurrency: Discussions on the impact of cryptocurrencies on the global financial system and potential regulations.
  3. Inclusive Growth: Emphasis on policies and initiatives to promote inclusive economic growth that benefits all segments of society.
  4. Climate Change: Addressing climate change challenges, sustainable development, and environmental conservation.
  5. Digital Public Infrastructure: Exploring opportunities and challenges in building digital infrastructure for public use.
  6. Women-Led Development: Promoting gender equality and women's empowerment in economic and social development.

These topics reflect the G20's commitment to addressing pressing global issues and fostering cooperation among member countries to achieve common goals.

üåü Outcomes

The G20 Summit 2023 in New Delhi resulted in several notable outcomes and agreements

  1. Africa's Membership: Africa joined the G20 organization and became a member from this year (2023), expanding the group's representation.
  2. Inclusive Growth: Leaders signed the New Delhi Leaders Declaration, emphasizing the importance of inclusive growth as a central goal of the summit.
  3. India-Middle East-Europe-USA Corridor: Leaders of India, the Middle East, Europe, and the USA proposed and passed a corridor initiative, potentially fostering economic cooperation and development.
  4. Global Biofuel Alliance: Member countries established the Global Biofuel Alliance, focusing on maximizing the use of biofuels to address energy and environmental challenges.

These outcomes reflect the collaborative efforts of G20 member countries to address global challenges, promote inclusive growth, and explore new avenues for economic and environmental sustainability.
